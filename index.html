Who We Are

A Legacy of Premium Craftsmanship since 2010

Based in Mussafah M-9, Abu Dhabi, VigiWoods Interior Design is a trusted name in carpentry, renovation, and maintenance services across the UAE.

We specialized in high-quality carpentry, bespoke furniture repairs, interior decorations, and comprehensive home renovations. From plumbing and electrical works to professional AC services and general repairs, we provide a one-stop-shop solution for all your residential and commercial needs.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality craftsmanship and tailored solutions that enhance spaces through reliability, customer satisfaction, and the use of premium materials, ensuring every project meets professional standards.
